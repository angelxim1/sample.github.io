Introduction

The respondent's profile section of this study aims to provide a comprehensive overview of the Grade-12 students' characteristics, including their sex, age, and Grade Weighted Average (GWA). Understanding the demographic and academic profile of the students is crucial for analyzing the relationship between social media usage and academic performance, as well as identifying the factors that influence their social media habits. This section serves as the foundation for investigating the extent of social media usage among the students and exploring its impact on their academic performance, study habits, and time management skills.
